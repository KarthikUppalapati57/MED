    Decision Gates

    Verified

    Trust score 80-100. User can continue to payment, plan selection, hierarchy setup, and workspace launch.

    Pending Review

    Trust score 50-79. User sees pending status; platform admin reviews supporting documents or business details.

    Failed

    Trust score below 50. Activation is blocked until the user corrects information or resubmits verification.
